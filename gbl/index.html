Description

Galactic Battle League 3015 (GBL) is a vehicular combat game by PanzerShark Games. Specifically, it is a hovertank arena battle game that is splitscreen multiplayer. It is being developed in Unity 5, and is using Xbox controllers.

History

Galactic Battle League 3015 started development in March 2015. The development cycle had a deadline of PAX Aus 2015, as it would be shown there. The project reached Alpha in early June, and is scheduled to reach Beta in mid September. Galactic Battle League has been showcased at Swinburne Open Day 2015 (Mid Year), as well as the KIOSC Careers of the future in August.

Features

  • Competitive Splitscreen Multiplayer
  • 2 different Vehicle Classes: Fast-paced Light Tanks, and Imposing Heavy Tanks
  • 4 Visually distinct Factions. Functionally Identical, but with different models, making 8 tank models
  • Cellshaded Sci-fi aesthetic
  • Original Soundtrack. Level tracks have factional overlays which help reinforce who’s winning
