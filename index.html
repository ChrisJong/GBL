History

PanzerShark Begins

In early March 2015, a group of Swinburne Games students formed a development team, known as PanzerShark Games, for their final year project. They knew that whatever they came up with would be shown at PAX Aus 2015, so they knew that they would try their absolute hardest to make something impressive. So they began work on Galactic Battle League 3015, a split-screen multiplayer vehicular combat game. The game is currently in the late stages of development, and the team is excitedly making preparations for PAX, and ensuring that their game will be complete by then.
